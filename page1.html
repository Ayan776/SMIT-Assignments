Pitch invasion incident will not be repeated : PCB

The Pakistan Cricket Board on Tuesday said it had taken serious note of the
security breach that occurred yesterday when a spectator entered the field of
play during the ICC Champions Trophy match between New Zealand and
Bangladesh.

Pitch invasion incidents are common in cricket and other sports across the globe when
overeager fans enter the playing field to try and meet their favourite players in person.

During the match in Rawalpindi, a fan entered the playing field and tried to hug New
Zealand player Rachin Ravindra. He held a photograph of Tehreek-i-Labbaik Pakistan
(TLP) chief Hafiz Saad Hussain Rizvi when he ran onto the pitch.
